Welcome to Umbra Space Industries!

Umbra Space Industries publishes a wide variety of mods for Kerbal Space Program, including mods for colonization, resource gathering, and various part packs. You can browse our offerings below. Included are links to the current release, the relevant KSP forum thread, and the related Github repo. Enjoy!

Want to keep up with the latest version? All USI mods use cybutek's Addon Version Checker (KSP-AVC) and check directly against the GitHub repo for any updates!

Like the mods and want to show your support (or just buy me a cup of coffee)? Click on the Patreon button below!
